TimeZ (Time Zone Reminder)

Origin: gelst13/ TimeZ/django-ver

is an application, designed to:

  • keep information about user's contacts time zone,
  • display their current time,
  • and make several operations with time,
based on user's local offset to UTC.

In Time operations it's possible to use UTC offset in format '+hh' of '-hhmm' (e.g. +02 or -0230) or TZ identifier from Olson database (check List of tz database time zones) {% endblock content %}
